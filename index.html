About Us

Splitting bills, not friendships.

We’ve all been there: the end of a great meal, trip, or group activity, followed by the awkward dance of "who owes who?" and the inevitable calculator struggle. We built Bug Slayers Bill Splitter to eliminate that stress entirely.

Our mission is simple: provide a fast, clean, and intuitive way to divide any group expense fairly and instantly. No sign-ups required, no confusing features—just clarity. Whether it's a simple dinner or a multi-currency group vacation, we ensure everyone knows where they stand, allowing you to get back to the fun part of life with friends and family.

Plus, you can refer to the attached google sheets link to have payment records at one page!
